"Heal depression through real action"

They say happiness will find you, but I think sadness will find you too.
But be careful in balancing the two, you may not be able to get through.
So much sadness brings you to darkest, it will turn stars to its dimmest.
Darkness is now what you seek, it paves way to depression at its peak.

Depression is not a joke, you feel like you're broke,
you look like you're choke, you suffer like you're stroke.
In the depths where shadows reign, Lies a world consumed by endless pain.
A silent ache that no words impart, A heavy burden, a shattered heart.

All the smiles on your face you have lied, when the pain in your heart you had to hide.
To your soul you are no more kind, if you become the slave of your own mind.
All the laughter’s on your mouth you’ve fake, when words left unspoken for others sake.
To your life you are no longer alive, if you let your heart be deprive.

Depression makes me mad; depression makes me sad.
Depression makes me bad, depression is unbelievingly hard.
It is a serious mental illness, it greatly affects your wellness,
you should avoid carelessness, to heal this severe sickness.

Healing implies kindness, healing means changes.
Healing takes time, but you can do it anytime.
Begin by having sense of satisfaction, Sense of balance and so on,
self-care of the whole person, and positive relation.

If you have problems don't run, rather face the light of sun,
whether you’re in one-on-one, but learn to enjoy and have fun.
Because life is not lame, it is a tricky game,
all people are facing the same, but react on different lane.

No matter how many times you’ve tried, no matter how many times you’ve cried.
No matter how many times you’ve fight, no matter how many times you’ve feel tight.
Believe in yourself that one day you’ll shine, after every hardship you’ll be fine.
Believe in God that one day you’ll rise, after every challenges there’s price.

So let compassion be the guiding voice, To heal the wounds, to make a choice.
For in the midst of shadows deep within, Hope persists—a new dawn begins.
Slowly, colors return to life's grand stage, Melodies of joy replace the silent cage.
Embracing imperfections, flaws, and scars, Reclaiming light from the dimmest stars.

Pagmamahal

Ang pagmamahal ay parang bulaklak,
Namumukadkad at namumulaklak,
Nagbibigay galak sa pusong wasak,
At pag-asa sa taong walang balak.

Sa mundong kay ganda at malawak,
Hindi mo mamamalayan ang patak,
Sa lakas ng luhang tumatagaktak,
Ng taong nagmamahal na winasak.

Ang pagmamahal ay parang bula,
Nawawala ito bigla bigla.
Minsan iniiwan ka sa gitna,
Sa mga taong hindi pa handa.

Sa mundong puno ng pag-aalinlangan,
Hindi mo lubos na maiintindihan,
Ang tunay at totoong nagmamahalan,
Kung hindi ka lalabas sa 'yong kulungan.

Ang pagmamahalan ay walang katiyakan,
Sa mundong puno ng pangangailangan,
'Di mo alam ika'y pinagsasamantalahan,
At inaabuso ang iyong kabutihan.

Love

Love is sometimes unpredictable,
Some find it as a miracle,
Some find it too painful,
Some find it beautiful.

When life is unfavorable,
It will make you comfortable.
When life is unstable,
It will make you responsible.

When love became too hurtful,
Stop it and be mindful,
'Cause you're not unbreakable,
You're a human who's vulnerable.

When love became too humble,
Keep it and be respectful,
'Cause you're not invisible,
You're a human who's grateful.
